# Future Anthem Future Anthem's all-in-one product for the real money gaming industry, Amplifier AI, delivers content personalization, real-time experiences, and dynamic audiences. Amplifier AI works seamlessly across sports, casino, and lottery. Amplifier AI and Braze's integration allows customers to enhance Braze's player profiles with industry-specific player attributes from Amplifier AI (e.g. favorite game, favorite team, engagement score, next bet recommendation, expected next bet, etc.) The integration is currently in Early Access.
